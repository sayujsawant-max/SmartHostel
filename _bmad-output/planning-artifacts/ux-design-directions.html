Key Takeaways

  • Direction 8 (Role-Adaptive Hybrid) scores highest overall but has the highest build cost — it's essentially four layouts sharing one design system.
  • Directions 1 (Clean) and 6 (Task-Driven) tie for best cost-efficiency — simple, consistent patterns that work adequately across all roles.
  • Direction 7 (Timeline) uniquely embodies the "provability" core principle but needs to be combined with action-oriented elements.
  • Directions 2, 3, 4, 5 each excel for one role but create awkward experiences for the others — good as ingredients, not standalone directions.
  • Recommended approach: Direction 8 (Hybrid) as the structural foundation, incorporating Timeline elements from Direction 7 for audit surfaces, within a 3-4 week MVP budget.
