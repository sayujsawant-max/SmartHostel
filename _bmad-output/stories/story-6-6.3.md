# Story 6.3: Warden Complaint & Leave Management Views

## Story

As a **warden**,
I want to view and manage all complaints and leaves with filtering,
So that I can handle my approval and oversight responsibilities efficiently.

## Status: Complete

## Acceptance Criteria

**AC1:** ComplaintsPage (from Story 5.2) shows complaints with status/priority/category filtering and assignment.

**AC2:** StudentsPage (leave management) shows all leaves with status filter, approve/reject actions.

**AC3:** Reject action includes optional reason input.

## Tasks

### Task 1: Build leave management UI (StudentsPage)
- Leave list with student info, dates, type, status badges
- Status filter dropdown (default: PENDING)
- Approve button, Reject with optional reason
